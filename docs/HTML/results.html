Results

Now, let's dive into our findings!

To retrieve the education data, we utilized the Census API. We examined the relationship between Bachelor's degree attainment and GDP p.c. among the counties of California. Additionally, we considered the number of educational institutions (kindergarten, primary, middle, high school, colleges, institutes, and universities) to measure the educational investments made by the counties.

The data were retrieved from three different datasets and then merged using the GEO_ID (the geographical variable used by the census).

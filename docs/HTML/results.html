Is it the same for everyone?

As a further step and attempt to define a valid theory, we analyse the impact of education on income of different races in the United States. Unfortunately, we have not found any data on education levels divided by race and county while we were able to retrieve data on salary per race in each region.

After merging and reshaping the data frame (you can read more about it here), we plotted out the results: they confirm our hypothesis.
